Beehook

Industrial Design, Logo

Beehook is a tie, belt, or scarf hanger designed in a hexagonal pattern to create a honeycomb. It was designed to build awareness of endangered bees (Colony Collapse Disorder) and its devastating effects on our future. This was created for a school project at the University of Houston.

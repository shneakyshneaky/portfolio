H&P OneTouch

Web Design

View Prototype

H&P is a petroleum drilling contractor focused on providing custom solutions and technologies. While undergoing a rebrand, H&P needed an interactive tool to help sales present products and allow clients to explore topics on their own in the future. The OneTouch Tool is an easily maneuverable web deck to navigate during sales conversations and used in trade-shows. In later phases, the tool will incorporate 3D visuals to display H&P technological advancements in automation and algorithms for drilling solutions.

My Role: UI Designer

I continued building out the Visual Design System based on the brand design from the Creative Director. I then merged the visual design and the UX Strategist’s wireframes into high-fidelity mockups and prototypes.
